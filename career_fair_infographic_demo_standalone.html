STM

Senior Trial Manager
SENIOR LEVEL

Oversees multiple trials within a specific portfolio (e.g., lymphoma, early diagnosis). First escalation point for complex issues.

Key Responsibilities

  • Protocol development with investigators
  • Portfolio leadership & oversight
  • Budget & contract management
  • Staff recruitment & resourcing
  • Sponsor & funder liaison

TM

Trial Manager
MID LEVEL

Coordinates study operations and first point of contact for Clinical Data Coordinators. Manages trial inbox and delegates tasks to coordinators.

Key Responsibilities

  • Site setup & recruitment monitoring
  • Write reports for trial oversight meetings
  • Arrange site initiation visits & investigator meetings
  • Regulatory & ethics approvals
  • Team coordination & task delegation

STS

Senior Trial Statistician
SENIOR LEVEL

Provides statistical leadership across multiple trials. Coordinates with trial and data management to resolve issues and meet deadlines. Mentors junior statisticians.

Key Responsibilities

  • Lead trial design (sample size and methodology)
  • Collaborate on funding applications and write publications
  • Lead statistical analysis plans and oversee interim and final analysis
  • Contribute to the trial protocol and database
  • Present reports to data monitoring committees
  • Mentor and supervise junior statisticians

TS

Trial Statistician
MID LEVEL

Produces statistical reports under the guidance of the senior statistician. Ensures data is correct and complete. Keeps up to date with statistical methodology.

Key Responsibilities

  • Provide statistical input at trial meetings
  • Ensure completeness and correctness of study data
  • Produce interim & final analysis reports
  • Develop the statistical analysis plan under guidance from the STS
  • Keep up to date with statistical methodology
  • Contribute to the trial protocol and database

CTM

Clinical Trial Monitor
MID LEVEL

Monitors site activity to ensure participant safety, data accuracy, and Good Clinical Practice compliance through on-site/remote visits.

Key Responsibilities

  • Conduct site visits to verify source data
  • Write visit reports
  • Review patient notes for unreported events
  • Drug & sample accountability
  • Serious adverse event reconciliation
  • Resolve monitoring issues & deviations

Key Responsibilities

  • Conduct audits & inspections
  • Maintain Quality Management System
  • Manage Standard Operating Procedures & corrective actions
  • Regulatory training compliance
  • Protocol amendment support
  • Document review & approval
