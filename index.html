About Me

I'm a Business Intelligence Analyst at the University of Southampton with a focus on healthcare analytics and clinical research data. I specialize in transforming complex datasets into clear, actionable insights that support strategic decision-making.

My work combines technical expertise in Python, Streamlit, and data engineering with deep understanding of healthcare research operations. I've built comprehensive analytics platforms analyzing portfolios worth £171.6M, developed automated dashboards that have demonstrated 122:1 ROI potential, and created tools that streamline clinical trial management across multiple sites.

Whether it's integrating satellite data for biomass prediction using machine learning, building real-time clinical trial monitoring systems, or creating career development resources, I approach every project with attention to both technical excellence and practical business impact.

I'm passionate about healthcare technology, data-driven decision making, and building solutions that make researchers' and administrators' work more efficient and impactful.
